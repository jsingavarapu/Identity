Citizenship can define identity?

  • Citizenship can define identity since the knowledge that your country is protecting you can give people a sense of belonging.
  • Citizenship can also give us a sense of identity if the person has pride in their country.
  • On the other hand there are many meanings of citizenship as the journal of case studies in education explains, “ In other words, different perspectives on citizenship define different perspectives on citizenship and define different relationships between citizenship and identity.” A bad experience with your country can give a sense of loss in identity.
